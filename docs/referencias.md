# Referencias

## ABM — Teoría y fundamentos

- **Macal, C. M., & North, M. J. (2009).** "Agent-based modeling and simulation." *Proceedings of the 2009 Winter Simulation Conference*. [Las seis propiedades operacionales de un agente.]
- **Wooldridge, M., & Jennings, N. R. (1995).** "Intelligent agents: theory and practice." *The Knowledge Engineering Review*, 10(2), 115–152.
- **Schelling, T. C. (1971).** "Dynamic models of segregation." *Journal of Mathematical Sociology*, 1(2), 143–186. [El ABM canónico de segregación residencial.]
- **Grimm, V., Railsback, S. F., Vincenot, C. E., et al. (2020).** "The ODD protocol for describing agent-based and other simulation models: A second update to improve clarity, replication, and structural realism." *Journal of Artificial Societies and Social Simulation*, 23(2), 7.
- **Railsback, S. F., & Grimm, V. (2019).** *Agent-Based and Individual-Based Modeling: A Practical Introduction* (2nd ed.). Princeton University Press.

## ENSO y climatología

- **NOAA Climate Prediction Center.** ONI (Oceanic Niño Index) — serie histórica 1950-presente. [https://www.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/data/indices/oni.ascii.txt](https://www.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/data/indices/oni.ascii.txt)
- **Hersbach, H., Bell, B., Berrisford, P., et al. (2020).** "The ERA5 global reanalysis." *Quarterly Journal of the Royal Meteorological Society*, 146(730), 1999–2049.
- **Poveda, G., Jaramillo, L., & Vallejo, L. F. (2014).** "Seasonal precipitation patterns along pathways of South American low-level jets and aerial rivers." *Water Resources Research*, 50(1), 98–118.
- **Hoyos, N., Escobar, J., Restrepo, J. C., Arango, A. M., & Ortiz, J. C. (2013).** "Impact of the 2010–2011 La Niña phenomenon in Colombia, South America: The human toll of an extreme weather event." *Applied Geography*, 39, 16–25.

## Sistema de Lorenz y caos

- **Lorenz, E. N. (1963).** "Deterministic nonperiodic flow." *Journal of the Atmospheric Sciences*, 20(2), 130–141.
- **Strogatz, S. H. (2015).** *Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos* (2nd ed.). CRC Press.

## Procesamiento de señales

- **Butterworth, S. (1930).** "On the theory of filter amplifiers." *Experimental Wireless and the Wireless Engineer*, 7, 536–541.
- **Scargle, J. D. (1982).** "Studies in astronomical time series analysis. II - Statistical aspects of spectral analysis of unevenly spaced data." *Astrophysical Journal*, 263, 835–853.

## Colombia — eventos históricos

- **IDEAM (2019).** *Estudio Nacional del Agua 2018*. Instituto de Hidrología, Meteorología y Estudios Ambientales.
- **Servicio Geológico Colombiano.** SIMMA — Sistema de Información de Movimientos en Masa. [https://simma.sgc.gov.co](https://simma.sgc.gov.co)

## Software

- **Mesa:** Kazil, J., Masad, D., & Crooks, A. (2020). "Utilizing Python for Agent-Based Modeling: The Mesa Framework." *Social, Cultural, and Behavioral Modeling* (SBP-BRiMS 2020). [https://mesa.readthedocs.io](https://mesa.readthedocs.io)
- **Solara:** Breddels, M., et al. (2023). *Solara: a pure Python, React-style web framework*. [https://solara.dev](https://solara.dev)
- **GeoPandas:** Jordahl, K., et al. (2021). *geopandas/geopandas: v0.9.0*. Zenodo.
- **xarray:** Hoyer, S., & Hamman, J. (2017). "xarray: N-D labeled arrays and datasets in Python." *Journal of Open Research Software*, 5(1).
